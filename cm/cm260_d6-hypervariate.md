
## D6 hypervariate

We will use the term *hypervariate* for data sets comprising 3 or more
quantitative variables.

Traditionally,

  - *univariate* data lie on a line
  - *bivariate* lie in a plane
  - *multivariate* two or more quantitative variables
  - *trivariate* three quantitative variables
  - *hypervariate* four or more quantitative variables
